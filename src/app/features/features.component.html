Features

Discover powerful features designed to simplify your loan process, accelerate approvals, and empower your DSA business to scale effortlessly.

Callback Management

Schedule and track client callbacks efficiently to ensure timely follow-ups and improved customer engagement, minimizing missed opportunities.

Lead Management

Efficiently capture and categorize leads by diverse loan products, enabling targeted workflows. Access and update detailed lead profiles with ease, supported by powerful advanced filtering and segmentation tools for enhanced decision-making.

Document Management

Securely collect, store, and verify borrower documents with seamless access controls and real-time tracking to streamline application processing and ensure compliance.

Multi Lender Management

Automatically match applicants with the most suitable banks and NBFCs based on eligibility, profile, and lending criteria.

CIBIL Report Integration

Instantly fetch and analyze CIBIL reports using API integration to evaluate creditworthiness, reduce delays, and make informed lending decisions in real-time.

Real-Time Sales Tracking

Monitor daily sales activity, conversion rates, and loan disbursals in real-time—empowering teams to stay proactive, data-driven, and aligned with their performance goals.

Smart DSA Lifecycle Management

Manage onboarding, activation, payouts, and performance reviews of DSAs with full transparency and operational efficiency—enhancing partner satisfaction and productivity.

Reporting & Analytics

Generate detailed performance reports, track KPIs, and analyze trends to support informed decision-making across teams and leadership levels.

Automated Email Communication

Streamline your outreach with automated emails for lead nurturing, status updates, reminders, and document requests—ensuring consistent communication and improved customer engagement.
